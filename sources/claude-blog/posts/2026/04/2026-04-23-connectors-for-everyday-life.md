# Claude 中新增的日常生活连接器

> 🌏︎ • New connectors in Claude for everyday life

> • 来源：Claude Blog，2026-04-23
> • 原文链接：https://claude.com/blog/connectors-for-everyday-life
> • 分类：Claude Blog

## 核心要点

- Claude 连接器（connectors）目录自 2025 年 7 月推出以来已增长至超过 200 个连接器。
- 此次扩展将连接能力延伸到工作之外的更多日常工具，新增 AllTrails、Audible、Booking.com、Instacart、Uber 等多个应用。
- 连接器现在会在对话中动态出现，Claude 会根据你的偏好和上下文主动推荐合适的应用。
- 当多个已连接应用都能帮助回答问题时，Claude 会同时展示并让你选择。
- Claude 无广告，对话中没有付费推荐或赞助回答；你的应用数据不会用于训练模型。
- 在代你预订或购买之前，Claude 被设计为会先与你确认。

## 正文

今天我们扩展了可以连接到 Claude 的范围。除了你已经在使用的工作工具之外，你现在还可以连接你一周中使用的各类应用，包括 AllTrails、Instacart、Audible、Tripadvisor、Intuit TurboTax 等等。

> 🌏︎ Today we’re expanding what you can connect to Claude. Alongside the work tools you already use, you can now connect the apps you use throughout your week, including AllTrails, Instacart, Audible, Tripadvisor, Intuit TurboTax, and more.

今天我们扩展了可以连接到 Claude 的内容。除了你已经在使用的办公工具之外，现在你还可以连接你在日常生活中使用的应用，包括 AllTrails、Instacart、Audible、Tripadvisor、Intuit TurboTax 等。

> 🌏︎ Today we’re expanding what you can connect to Claude. Alongside the work tools you already use, you can now connect the apps you use throughout your week, including AllTrails, Instacart, Audible, Tripadvisor, Intuit TurboTax, and more.

### 人们如何使用连接器（connector）

> How people have been using connectors

自2025年7月推出以来，Claude目录已增长至超过200个连接器，涵盖设计、金融、生产力和健康等领域的热门应用，每天有数百万人使用。我们发现，人们经常连接多个应用，并在与Claude的同一次对话中协同使用它们。例如，产品经理可以从Amplitude拉取一份查询结果，将其转化为Canva演示文稿，再把链接放入Asana分享给团队，整个过程都无需离开对话。

> 🌏︎ Since launching in July 2025, the Claude directory has grown to over 200 connectors, spanning popular apps for design, finance, productivity, and health used by millions of people every day. We’ve found that people often connect multiple apps and use them together in a single conversation with Claude. For example, a product manager will pull a query from Amplitude, turn it into a Canva deck, and drop the link into Asana for the team, all without leaving the conversation.

此次扩展将这一功能延伸到你在工作之外使用的更多工具。从今天起，Claude可连接AllTrails、Audible、Booking.com、Instacart、Intuit Credit Karma、Intuit TurboTax、Resy、Spotify、StubHub、Taskrabbit、Thumbtack、Tripadvisor、Uber、Uber Eats和Viator，更多连接器即将推出。

> 🌏︎ This expansion extends that functionality to more of the tools you use outside of work. Starting today, Claude connects to AllTrails, Audible, Booking.com, Instacart, Intuit Credit Karma, Intuit TurboTax, Resy, Spotify, StubHub, Taskrabbit, Thumbtack, Tripadvisor, Uber, Uber Eats, and Viator, with more on the way.

### 连接器（Connectors）在对话中动态出现

> Connectors dynamically show up in conversations

我们还改变了连接器在对话中出现的方式。Claude 现在会根据你正在做的事情推荐合适的应用，比如查找预订、添加到购物车，或识别航班。它依据的是你告诉过它的信息：你的偏好、你的上下文、你的对话。

> 🌏︎ We’ve also changed how connectors show up in conversations. Claude now suggests the right app for what you’re doing, like finding a reservation, adding to a grocery cart, or identifying a flight. It’s working from what you’ve told it: your preferences, your context, your conversation.

这一切都在你的对话内部完成。让 Claude 推荐一条周末徒步路线，AllTrails 就会呈现附近符合你偏好的路线。然后你可以在不离开对话的情况下，通过要求更短、更有风景或更适合带狗的路线来优化推荐。当有不止一个已连接的应用可以帮助回答你的问题时，Claude 会把它们全部显示出来，让你选择。

> 🌏︎ This all happens inside your conversation. Ask Claude to recommend a weekend hike, and AllTrails will surface trails nearby that match your preferences. You can then refine the recommendations by asking for something shorter, more scenic, or dog-friendly without leaving the thread. When more than one connected app could help answer your question, Claude shows them all and lets you choose.

### 你掌控所见与所享

> You control what you see and share

Claude 没有广告，并将保持如此。与 Claude 的对话中不会有付费植入或赞助答案。当两个连接器（connector）都能帮上忙时，你会同时看到它们，并按对你最有用的程度排序。

> 🌏︎ Claude is ad-free and will stay that way. There are no paid placements or sponsored answers in conversations with Claude. When two connectors could help, you see both, ranked by what’s most useful to you.

连接某项服务后，Claude 便能代表你访问该服务。该应用中的数据不会用于训练我们的模型，应用也看不到你与 Claude 的其他对话。你也可以随时断开连接。

> 🌏︎ Connecting a service gives Claude access to it on your behalf. Your data from that app isn’t used to train our models, and the app doesn’t see your other conversations with Claude. You can also disconnect it at any time.

Claude 会推荐连接器并给出建议。但你始终掌控它的行动：在它代表你预订或购买之前，它被设计为会先与你确认。

> 🌏︎ Claude suggests connectors and makes recommendations. But you stay in control of its actions: before it books or purchases something on your behalf, it’s designed to check with you first.

### 开始使用

> Getting started

连接的应用越多，Claude 能做的事就越多。Claude 会在你工作时推荐相关的连接器（connectors），你在桌面端只需点击一次、在移动端只需轻点几下即可安装。一旦连接，该服务便会在每次对话中可用。

> 🌏︎ The more apps you connect, the more Claude can do. Claude suggests relevant connectors as you work, which you can install with a click on desktop or a few taps on mobile. Once connected, the service is available in every conversation.

连接器在所有套餐中均可使用，移动端目前处于测试版（beta）。完整列表见 claude.ai/directory/connectors。如果你构建了在 Claude 中有用的产品，可在此提交到我们的目录。

> 🌏︎ Connectors are available on all plans, with mobile in beta. See the full list at claude.ai/directory/connectors . If you build a product that would be useful in Claude, submit it to our directory here .

关于如何开始使用连接器，以及将数据源连接到 Claude 时的安全与隐私最佳实践，更多信息请访问我们的帮助中心。

> 🌏︎ For more information on getting started with connectors, along with security and privacy best practices when connecting data sources to Claude, visit our Help Center .
