# 元强化学习

> Meta Reinforcement Learning

> 来源：Lil'Log / Lilian Weng，2019-06-23
> 原文链接：https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/
> 分类：人工智能 / 元强化学习

## 核心要点

- 元强化学习旨在开发能够快速高效解决未见强化学习任务的智能体。
- 一个优秀的元学习模型应能泛化到训练期间未曾遇到的新任务或新环境。
- Hochreiter等人在2001年提出了使用带有LSTM单元的循环网络进行元学习的早期思想。
- Wang等人和Duan等人在2016/2017年提出了现代元强化学习（Meta-RL或RL^2），通过在MDPs分布上训练实现快速任务学习。
- 元强化学习与普通强化学习的主要区别在于，其策略观察中包含了上一个奖励和上一个动作。
- 元强化学习的三个关键组成部分是：具有记忆的模型、元学习算法和MDPs的分布。
- 元强化学习的元学习算法包括优化模型权重、元学习超参数、元学习损失函数和元学习探索策略。
- 情景控制通过保留过去事件的明确记录并直接用作决策参考，以提高强化学习的样本效率和泛化能力。
- 训练任务的获取可以通过领域随机化、进化算法（如POET）或无监督方法（如随机奖励学习和DIAYN）来实现。
- 元强化学习是构建通用人工智能的重要一步，旨在使智能体无需大量人工干预即可解决各种强化学习问题。

> In my earlier post on [meta-learning](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/), the problem is mainly defined in the context of few-shot classification. Here I would like to explore more into cases when we try to “meta-learn” [Reinforcement Learning (RL)](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/) tasks by developing an agent that can solve unseen tasks fast and efficiently.

回顾一下，一个好的元学习模型有望泛化到训练期间从未遇到过的新任务或新环境。适应过程，本质上是一个*小型学习会话*，在测试时发生，对新配置的接触有限。即使没有任何显式微调（没有对可训练变量进行梯度反向传播），元学习模型也能自主调整内部隐藏状态以进行学习。

> To recap, a good meta-learning model is expected to generalize to new tasks or new environments that have never been encountered during training. The adaptation process, essentially a *mini learning session*, happens at test with limited exposure to the new configurations. Even without any explicit fine-tuning (no gradient backpropagation on trainable variables), the meta-learning model autonomously adjusts internal hidden states to learn.

训练强化学习算法有时可能非常困难。如果元学习智能体能够变得如此智能，以至于可解决的未见任务的分布变得极其广泛，那么我们正朝着[通用方法](http://incompleteideas.net/IncIdeas/BitterLesson.html)迈进——本质上是构建一个“大脑”，它可以在没有太多人为干预或手动特征工程的情况下解决各种强化学习问题。听起来很棒，对吧？💖

> Training RL algorithms can be notoriously difficult sometimes. If the meta-learning agent could become so smart that the distribution of solvable unseen tasks grows extremely broad, we are on track towards [general purpose methods](http://incompleteideas.net/IncIdeas/BitterLesson.html) — essentially building a “brain” which would solve all kinds of RL problems without much human interference or manual feature engineering. Sounds amazing, right? 💖

### 元强化学习的起源

> On the Origin of Meta-RL

#### 回到2001年

> Back in 2001

我偶然发现了一篇2001年由[Hochreiter et al.](http://snowedin.net/tmp/Hochreiter2001.pdf)在阅读[Wang et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1611.05763.pdf)时。尽管这个想法是为监督学习提出的，但它与当前元强化学习的方法有许多相似之处。

> I encountered a paper  written in 2001 by [Hochreiter et al.](http://snowedin.net/tmp/Hochreiter2001.pdf) when reading [Wang et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1611.05763.pdf). Although the idea was proposed for supervised learning, there are so many resemblances to the current approach to meta-RL.

![The meta-learning system consists of the supervisory and the subordinate systems. The subordinate system is a recurrent neural network that takes as input both the observation at the current time step, $x\_t$ and the label at the last time step, $y\_{t-1}$. (Image source: Hochreiter et al., 2001 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/Hochreiter-meta-learning.png)

Hochreiter 的元学习模型是一个带有 LSTM 单元的循环网络。LSTM 是一个很好的选择，因为它能够内化输入历史并通过[BPTT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backpropagation_through_time)。训练数据包含$K$个序列，每个序列由$N$个样本组成，这些样本由目标函数$f_k(.), k=1, \dots, K$生成，

> Hochreiter’s meta-learning model is a recurrent network with LSTM cell. LSTM is a good choice because it can internalize a history of inputs and tune its own weights effectively through [BPTT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backpropagation_through_time). The training data contains $K$ sequences and each sequence is consist of $N$ samples generated by a target function $f_k(.), k=1, \dots, K$,

$$
\{\text{input: }(\mathbf{x}^k_i, \mathbf{y}^k_{i-1}) \to \text{label: }\mathbf{y}^k_i\}_{i=1}^N
\text{ where }\mathbf{y}^k_i = f_k(\mathbf{x}^k_i)
$$

值得注意的是，*最后一个标签* $\mathbf{y}^k_{i-1}$ 也作为一个辅助输入提供，以便函数可以学习所呈现的映射。

> Noted that *the last label* $\mathbf{y}^k_{i-1}$ is also provided as an auxiliary input so that the function can learn the presented mapping.

在解码二维二次函数的实验中，$a x_1^2 + b x_2^2 + c x_1 x_2 + d x_1 + e x_2 + f$，其系数$a$ -$f$从[-1, 1]中随机采样，这个元学习系统在只看到大约35个示例后就能够近似该函数。

> In the experiment of decoding two-dimensional quadratic functions, $a x_1^2 + b x_2^2 + c x_1 x_2 + d x_1 + e x_2 + f$, with coefficients $a$ -$f$ are randomly sampled from [-1, 1], this meta-learning system was able to approximate the function after seeing only ~35 examples.

#### 2016年的提案

> Proposal in 2016

在深度学习的现代，[Wang 等人](https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.05763) (2016) 和 [Duan 等人](https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.02779) (2017) 同时提出了非常相似的**元强化学习**（Meta-RL）思想（在第二篇论文中被称为**RL^2**）。元强化学习模型在MDPs的分布上进行训练，在测试时，它能够快速学习解决新任务。元强化学习的目标是雄心勃勃的，它向通用算法又迈进了一步。

> In the modern days of DL, [Wang et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.05763) (2016) and [Duan et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.02779) (2017) simultaneously proposed the very similar idea of **Meta-RL** (it is called **RL^2** in the second paper). A meta-RL model is trained over a distribution of MDPs, and at test time, it is able to learn to solve a new task quickly. The goal of meta-RL is ambitious, taking one step further towards general algorithms.

### 定义元强化学习

> Define Meta-RL

*元强化学习*，简而言之，就是在[强化学习](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/)领域进行[元学习](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/)。通常，训练和测试任务不同，但都来自同一类问题；例如，论文中的实验包括具有不同奖励概率的多臂老虎机、具有不同布局的迷宫、模拟器中具有不同物理参数的相同机器人等等。

> *Meta Reinforcement Learning*, in short, is to do [meta-learning](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/) in the field of [reinforcement learning](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/). Usually the train and test tasks are different but drawn from the same family of problems; i.e., experiments in the papers included multi-armed bandit with different reward probabilities, mazes with different layouts, same robots but with different physical parameters in simulator, and many others.

#### 公式化

> Formulation

假设我们有一个任务分布，每个任务都公式化为一个[MDP](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#markov-decision-processes)（马尔可夫决策过程），$M_i \in \mathcal{M}$。一个MDP由一个四元组决定，$M_i= \langle \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P_i, R_i \rangle$：

> Let’s say we have a distribution of tasks, each formularized as an [MDP](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#markov-decision-processes) (Markov Decision Process), $M_i \in \mathcal{M}$. An MDP is determined by a 4-tuple, $M_i= \langle \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P_i, R_i \rangle$:

| 符号 | 含义 |
| --- | --- |
| $\mathcal{S}$ | 一组状态。 |
| $\mathcal{A}$ | 一组动作。 |
| $P_i: \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \times \mathcal{S} \to \mathbb{R}_{+}$ | 转移概率函数。 |
| $R_i: \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \to \mathbb{R}$ | 奖励函数。 |

> 英文原表 / English original

| Symbol | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| $\mathcal{S}$ | A set of states. |
| $\mathcal{A}$ | A set of actions. |
| $P_i: \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \times \mathcal{S} \to \mathbb{R}_{+}$ | Transition probability function. |
| $R_i: \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \to \mathbb{R}$ | Reward function. |

(RL^2 论文增加了一个额外的参数，horizon $T$，到 MDP 元组中，以强调每个 MDP 都应该有一个有限的 horizon。）

> (RL^2 paper adds an extra parameter, horizon $T$, into the MDP tuple to emphasize that each MDP should have a finite horizon.)

请注意，上面使用了共同的状态 $\mathcal{S}$ 和动作空间 $\mathcal{A}$，这样（随机）策略：$\pi_\theta: \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \to \mathbb{R}_{+}$ 将获得在不同任务中兼容的输入。测试任务从相同的分布 $\mathcal{M}$ 或略微修改的版本中采样。

> Note that common state $\mathcal{S}$ and action space $\mathcal{A}$ are used above, so that a (stochastic) policy: $\pi_\theta: \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \to \mathbb{R}_{+}$ would get inputs compatible across different tasks. The test tasks are sampled from the same distribution $\mathcal{M}$ or slightly modified version.

![Illustration of meta-RL, containing two optimization loops. The outer loop samples a new environment in every iteration and adjusts parameters that determine the agent's behavior. In the inner loop, the agent interacts with the environment and optimizes for the maximal reward. (Image source: Botvinick, et al. 2019 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/meta-RL-illustration.png)

#### 与 RL 的主要区别

> Main Differences from RL

元强化学习（meta-RL）的整体配置与普通强化学习算法非常相似，不同之处在于**上一个奖励**`r_{t-1}`和**上一个动作**`a_{t-1}`除了当前状态之外，还被纳入策略观察中`s_t`。

英文原文：The overall configure of meta-RL is very similar to an ordinary RL algorithm, except that the last reward `r_{t-1}` and the last action `a_{t-1}` are also incorporated into the policy observation in addition to the current state `s_t`.

• 在强化学习中：$\pi_\theta(s_t) \to$ 一个关于$\mathcal{A}$的分布

• 在元强化学习中：$\pi_\theta(a_{t-1}, r_{t-1}, s_t) \to$ 一个关于 $\mathcal{A}$ 的分布

英文原文：

• In RL: $\pi_\theta(s_t) \to$  a distribution over $\mathcal{A}$

• In meta-RL: $\pi_\theta(a_{t-1}, r_{t-1}, s_t) \to$  a distribution over $\mathcal{A}$

这种设计的目的是将历史信息输入模型，以便策略能够内化当前MDP中状态、奖励和动作之间的动态，并相应地调整其策略。这与[Hochreiter的系统](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#back-in-2001)中的设置非常吻合。元强化学习和RL^2都实现了LSTM策略，并且LSTM的隐藏状态作为*记忆*来跟踪轨迹的特征。由于策略是循环的，因此无需显式地将最后一个状态作为输入。

> The intention of this design is to feed a history into the model so that the policy can internalize the dynamics between states, rewards, and actions in the current MDP and adjust its strategy accordingly. This is well aligned with the setup in [Hochreiter’s system](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#back-in-2001). Both meta-RL and RL^2 implemented an LSTM policy and the LSTM’s hidden states serve as a *memory* for tracking characteristics of the trajectories. Because the policy is recurrent, there is no need to feed the last state as inputs explicitly.

训练过程如下：

> The training procedure works as follows:

1\. 采样一个新的MDP，$M_i \sim \mathcal{M}$；

2\. **重置模型隐藏状态**；

3\. 收集多条轨迹并更新模型权重；

4\. 从步骤1重复。

英文原文：

1\. Sample a new MDP, $M_i \sim \mathcal{M}$;

2\. **Reset the hidden state** of the model;

3\. Collect multiple trajectories and update the model weights;

4\. Repeat from step 1.

The observation is fed into the LSTM either as a one-hot vector or as an embedding vector after passed through an encoder model. (Image source: Wang et al., 2016 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/L2RL.png)

![As described in the RL^2 paper, illustration of the procedure of the model interacting with a series of MDPs in training time . (Image source: Duan et al., 2017 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/RL_2.png)

#### 关键组成部分

> Key Components

元强化学习（Meta-RL）中有三个关键组成部分：

> There are three key components in Meta-RL:

> ⭐ **具有记忆的模型**
>
>
> 循环神经网络维护一个隐藏状态。因此，它可以通过在rollout期间更新隐藏状态来获取和记忆当前任务的知识。没有记忆，元强化学习将无法工作。

> ⭐ **A Model with Memory**
>
>
> A recurrent neural network maintains a hidden state. Thus, it could acquire and memorize the knowledge about the current task by updating the hidden state during rollouts. Without memory, meta-RL would not work.

> ⭐ **元学习算法**
>
>
> 元学习算法指的是我们如何更新模型权重，以优化在测试时快速解决一个未见任务的目的。在Meta-RL和RL^2两篇论文中，元学习算法都是LSTM的普通梯度下降更新，并在MDPs切换之间重置隐藏状态。

> ⭐ **Meta-learning Algorithm**
>
>
> A meta-learning algorithm refers to how we can update the model weights to optimize for the purpose of solving an unseen task fast at test time. In both Meta-RL and RL^2 papers, the meta-learning algorithm is the ordinary gradient descent update of LSTM with hidden state reset between a switch of MDPs.

> ⭐ **MDPs的分布**
>
>
> 虽然智能体在训练期间会接触到各种环境和任务，但它必须学习如何适应不同的MDPs。

> ⭐ **A Distribution of MDPs**
>
>
> While the agent is exposed to a variety of environments and tasks during training, it has to learn how to adapt to different MDPs.

根据[Botvinick et al.](https://www.cell.com/action/showPdf?pii=S1364-6613%2819%2930061-0) (2019)的说法，强化学习训练缓慢的一个原因是*弱[归纳偏置](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inductive_bias)*（=“学习器用来预测给定未遇到输入的输出的一组假设”）。作为一条通用的机器学习规则，具有弱归纳偏置的学习算法能够掌握更广泛的变异，但通常样本效率较低。因此，通过更强的归纳偏置来缩小假设范围有助于提高学习速度。

> According to [Botvinick et al.](https://www.cell.com/action/showPdf?pii=S1364-6613%2819%2930061-0) (2019), one source of slowness in RL training is *weak [inductive bias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inductive_bias)* ( = “a set of assumptions that the learner uses to predict outputs given inputs that it has not encountered”). As a general ML rule, a learning algorithm with weak inductive bias will be able to master a wider range of variance, but usually, will be less sample-efficient. Therefore, to narrow down the hypotheses with stronger inductive biases help improve the learning speed.

在元强化学习中，我们从*任务分布*中施加特定类型的归纳偏置，并将其存储在*记忆*中。在测试时采用哪种归纳偏置取决于*算法*。这三个关键组成部分共同描绘了元强化学习的一个引人注目的观点：调整循环网络的权重很慢，但它允许模型通过其内部活动动态中实现的自身强化学习算法快速解决新任务。

> In meta-RL, we impose certain types of inductive biases from the *task distribution* and store them in *memory*. Which inductive bias to adopt at test time depends on the *algorithm*. Together, these three key components depict a compelling view of meta-RL: Adjusting the weights of a recurrent network is slow but it allows the model to work out a new task fast with its own RL algorithm implemented in its internal activity dynamics.

元强化学习有趣且不令人意外地与Jeff Clune (2019)的[AI-GAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.10985)（“AI生成算法”）论文中的思想相吻合。他提出，构建通用人工智能的一种有效方法是使学习尽可能自动化。AI-GAs方法包括三个支柱：（1）元学习架构，（2）元学习算法，以及（3）用于有效学习的自动生成环境。

> Meta-RL interestingly and not very surprisingly matches the ideas in the [AI-GAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.10985) (“AI-Generating Algorithms”) paper by Jeff Clune (2019). He proposed that one efficient way towards building general AI is to make learning as automatic as possible. The AI-GAs approach involves three pillars: (1) meta-learning architectures, (2) meta-learning algorithms, and (3) automatically generated environments for effective learning.

设计好的循环网络架构这个话题有点太宽泛，不适合在这里讨论，所以我将跳过它。接下来，让我们进一步探讨另外两个组成部分：元强化学习背景下的元学习算法以及如何获取各种训练MDPs。

> The topic of designing good recurrent network architectures is a bit too broad to be discussed here, so I will skip it. Next, let’s look further into another two components: meta-learning algorithms in the context of meta-RL and how to acquire a variety of training MDPs.

### 元强化学习的元学习算法

> Meta-Learning Algorithms for Meta-RL

#### 优化元学习的模型权重

> Optimizing Model Weights for Meta-learning

MAML ([Finn, et al. 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.03400)) 和 Reptile ([Nichol et al., 2018](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.02999)) 都是更新模型参数以在新任务上实现良好泛化性能的方法。请参阅之前关于 MAML 和 Reptile 的帖子中的[章节](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/#optimization-based)。

> Both MAML ([Finn, et al. 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.03400)) and Reptile ([Nichol et al., 2018](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.02999)) are methods on updating model parameters in order to achieve good generalization performance on new tasks. See an earlier post [section](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/#optimization-based) on MAML and Reptile.

#### 元学习超参数

> Meta-learning Hyperparameters

RL 问题中的[回报](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#value-function)函数，$G_t^{(n)}$ 或 $G_t^\lambda$，涉及一些通常凭经验设置的超参数，例如折扣因子 [\gamma](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#value-function) 和自举参数 [\lambda](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#combining-td-and-mc-learning)。元梯度强化学习 ([Xu et al., 2018](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/7507-meta-gradient-reinforcement-learning.pdf)) 将它们视为*元参数*，$\eta=\{\gamma, \lambda \}$，可以在智能体与环境交互时*在线*调整和学习。因此，回报成为 $\eta$ 的函数，并随着时间动态地适应特定任务。

> The [return](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#value-function) function in an RL problem, $G_t^{(n)}$ or $G_t^\lambda$, involves a few hyperparameters that are often set heuristically, like the discount factor [\gamma](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#value-function) and the bootstrapping parameter [\lambda](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-02-19-rl-overview/#combining-td-and-mc-learning).
> Meta-gradient RL ([Xu et al., 2018](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/7507-meta-gradient-reinforcement-learning.pdf)) considers them as *meta-parameters*, $\eta=\{\gamma, \lambda \}$, that can be tuned and learned *online* while an agent is interacting with the environment. Therefore, the return becomes a function of $\eta$ and dynamically adapts itself to a specific task over time.

$$
\begin{aligned}
G_\eta^{(n)}(\tau_t) &= R_{t+1} + \gamma R_{t+2} + \dots + \gamma^{n-1}R_{t+n} + \gamma^n v_\theta(s_{t+n}) & \scriptstyle{\text{; n-step return}} \\
G_\eta^{\lambda}(\tau_t) &= (1-\lambda) \sum_{n=1}^\infty \lambda^{n-1} G_\eta^{(n)} & \scriptstyle{\text{; λ-return, mixture of n-step returns}}
\end{aligned}
$$

在训练期间，我们希望使用梯度更新策略参数，作为所有现有信息的函数，$\theta’ = \theta + f(\tau, \theta, \eta)$，其中 $\theta$ 是当前模型权重，$\tau$ 是轨迹序列，$\eta$ 是元参数。

> During training, we would like to update the policy parameters with gradients as a function of all the information in hand, $\theta’ = \theta + f(\tau, \theta, \eta)$, where $\theta$ are the current model weights, $\tau$ is a sequence of trajectories, and $\eta$ are the meta-parameters.

同时，假设我们有一个元目标函数 $J(\tau, \theta, \eta)$ 作为性能度量。训练过程遵循在线交叉验证的原则，使用一系列连续的经验：

> Meanwhile, let’s say we have a meta-objective function $J(\tau, \theta, \eta)$ as a performance measure. The training process follows the principle of online cross-validation, using a sequence of consecutive experiences:

1\. 从参数 $\theta$ 开始，策略 $\pi_\theta$ 在第一批样本 $\tau$ 上更新，得到 $\theta’$。

2\. 然后我们继续运行策略 $\pi_{\theta’}$ 以收集一组新的经验 $\tau’$，只是按时间顺序连续地遵循 $\tau$。性能以 $J(\tau’, \theta’, \bar{\eta})$ 衡量，其中元参数 $\bar{\eta}$ 是固定的。

3\. 元目标 $J(\tau’, \theta’, \bar{\eta})$ 对 $\eta$ 的梯度用于更新 $\eta$：

英文原文：

1\. Starting with parameter $\theta$, the policy $\pi_\theta$ is updated on the first batch of samples $\tau$, resulting in $\theta’$.

2\. Then we continue running the policy $\pi_{\theta’}$ to collect a new set of experiences $\tau’$, just following $\tau$ consecutively in time. The performance is measured as $J(\tau’, \theta’, \bar{\eta})$ with a fixed meta-parameter $\bar{\eta}$.

3\. The gradient of meta-objective $J(\tau’, \theta’, \bar{\eta})$ w.r.t. $\eta$ is used to update $\eta$:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\Delta \eta
&= -\beta \frac{\partial J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta})}{\partial \eta} \\
&= -\beta \frac{\partial J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta})}{\partial \theta'} \frac{d\theta'}{d\eta} & \scriptstyle{\text{ ; single variable chain rule.}} \\
&= -\beta \frac{\partial J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta})}{\partial \theta'} \frac{\partial (\theta + f(\tau, \theta, \eta))}{\partial\eta}  \\
&= -\beta \frac{\partial J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta})}{\partial \theta'} \Big(\frac{d\theta}{d\eta} + \frac{\partial f(\tau, \theta, \eta)}{\partial\theta}\frac{d\theta}{d\eta} + \frac{\partial f(\tau, \theta, \eta)}{\partial\eta}\frac{d\eta}{d\eta} \Big) & \scriptstyle{\text{; multivariable chain rule.}}\\
&= -\beta \frac{\partial J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta})}{\partial \theta'} \Big( \color{red}{\big(\mathbf{I} + \frac{\partial f(\tau, \theta, \eta)}{\partial\theta}\big)}\frac{d\theta}{d\eta} + \frac{\partial f(\tau, \theta, \eta)}{\partial\eta}\Big) & \scriptstyle{\text{; secondary gradient term in red.}}
\end{aligned}
$$

其中 $\beta$ 是 $\eta$ 的学习率。

> where $\beta$ is the learning rate for $\eta$.

元梯度强化学习算法通过将次级梯度项设置为零来简化计算，$\mathbf{I} + \partial g(\tau, \theta, \eta)/\partial\theta = 0$ — 这种选择倾向于元参数$\eta$对参数$\theta$的即时影响。最终我们得到：

> The meta-gradient RL algorithm simplifies the computation by setting the secondary gradient term to zero, $\mathbf{I} + \partial g(\tau, \theta, \eta)/\partial\theta = 0$ — this choice prefers the immediate effect of the meta-parameters $\eta$ on the parameters $\theta$. Eventually we get:

$$
\Delta \eta = -\beta \frac{\partial J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta})}{\partial \theta'} \frac{\partial f(\tau, \theta, \eta)}{\partial\eta}
$$

论文中的实验采用了与$TD(\lambda)$算法相同的元目标函数，最小化近似值函数$v_\theta(s)$与$\lambda$回报之间的误差：

> Experiments in the paper adopted the meta-objective function same as $TD(\lambda)$ algorithm, minimizing the error between the approximated value function $v_\theta(s)$ and the $\lambda$ -return:

$$
\begin{aligned}
J(\tau, \theta, \eta) &= (G^\lambda_\eta(\tau) - v_\theta(s))^2 \\
J(\tau', \theta', \bar{\eta}) &= (G^\lambda_{\bar{\eta}}(\tau') - v_{\theta'}(s'))^2
\end{aligned}
$$

#### 元学习损失函数

> Meta-learning the Loss Function

在策略梯度算法中，通过沿着估计梯度的方向更新策略参数$\theta$来最大化预期总奖励（[Schulman et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.02438)），

> In policy gradient algorithms, the expected total reward is maximized by updating the policy parameters $\theta$ in the direction of estimated gradient ([Schulman et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.02438)),

$$
g = \mathbb{E}[\sum_{t=0}^\infty \Psi_t \nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta (a_t \mid s_t)]
$$

其中$\Psi_t$的候选包括轨迹回报$G_t$、Q值$Q(s_t, a_t)$或优势值$A(s_t, a_t)$。策略梯度对应的替代损失函数可以逆向工程得到：

> where the candidates for $\Psi_t$ include the trajectory return $G_t$, the Q value $Q(s_t, a_t)$, or the advantage value $A(s_t, a_t)$. The corresponding surrogate loss function for the policy gradient can be reverse-engineered:

$$
L_\text{pg} = \mathbb{E}[\sum_{t=0}^\infty \Psi_t \log \pi_\theta (a_t \mid s_t)]
$$

这个损失函数是对轨迹历史的度量，$(s_0, a_0, r_0, \dots, s_t, a_t, r_t, \dots)$。**演化策略梯度** (**EPG**; [Houthooft, et al, 2018](https://papers.nips.cc/paper/7785-evolved-policy-gradients.pdf)) 通过将策略梯度损失函数定义为基于智能体过往经验的时间卷积（一维卷积）而更进一步，$L_\phi$。损失函数网络的参数 `\phi` 以智能体能够获得更高回报的方式进行演化。

英文原文：This loss function is a measure over a history of trajectories, 

$(s_0, a_0, r_0, \dots, s_t, a_t, r_t, \dots)$. Evolved Policy Gradient (EPG; [Houthooft, et al, 2018](https://papers.nips.cc/paper/7785-evolved-policy-gradients.pdf)) takes a step further by defining the policy gradient loss function as a temporal convolution (1-D convolution) over the agent’s past experience, 

$L_\phi$. The parameters `\phi` of the loss function network are evolved in a way that an agent can achieve higher returns.

类似于许多元学习算法，EPG 有两个优化循环：

> Similar to many meta-learning algorithms, EPG has two optimization loops:

• 在内部循环中，一个智能体学习改进其策略$\pi_\theta$。

• 在外层循环中，模型更新参数$\phi$的损失函数$L_\phi$。因为没有明确的方法来写出回报与损失之间的可微分方程，EPG 转向了[进化策略](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolution_strategy)（ES）。

英文原文：

• In the internal loop, an agent learns to improve its policy $\pi_\theta$.

• In the outer loop, the model updates the parameters $\phi$ of the loss function $L_\phi$. Because there is no explicit way to write down a differentiable equation between the return and the loss, EPG turned to [Evolutionary Strategies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolution_strategy) (ES).

一个普遍的想法是训练一组$N$智能体，每个智能体都使用损失函数$L_{\phi + \sigma \epsilon_i}$进行参数化，其中参数为$\phi$并添加了小的 Gaussian 噪声$\epsilon_i \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \mathbf{I})$，其标准差为$\sigma$在内循环训练期间，EPG 会跟踪经验历史，并根据损失函数更新策略参数$L_{\phi + \sigma\epsilon_i}$，针对每个智能体：

> A general idea is to train a population of $N$ agents, each of them is trained with the loss function $L_{\phi + \sigma \epsilon_i}$ parameterized with $\phi$ added with a small Gaussian noise $\epsilon_i \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \mathbf{I})$ of standard deviation $\sigma$. During the inner loop’s training, EPG tracks a history of experience and updates the policy parameters according to the loss function $L_{\phi + \sigma\epsilon_i}$ for each agent:

$$
\theta_i \leftarrow \theta - \alpha_\text{in} \nabla_\theta L_{\phi + \sigma \epsilon_i} (\pi_\theta, \tau_{t-K, \dots, t})
$$

其中 $\alpha_\text{in}$ 是内循环的学习率，$\tau_{t-K, \dots, t}$ 是一系列 $M$ 转换，直到当前时间步 $t$。

> where $\alpha_\text{in}$ is the learning rate of the inner loop and $\tau_{t-K, \dots, t}$ is a sequence of $M$ transitions up to the current time step $t$.

一旦内循环策略足够成熟，就会通过在多个随机采样的轨迹上计算平均回报 $\bar{G}_{\phi+\sigma\epsilon_i}$ 来评估该策略。最终，我们能够根据 [NES](http://blog.otoro.net/2017/10/29/visual-evolution-strategies/) 数值估计 $\phi$ 的梯度（[Salimans et al, 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.03864)）。在重复此过程的同时，策略参数 $\theta$ 和损失函数权重 $\phi$ 会同时更新，以实现更高的回报。

> Once the inner loop policy is mature enough, the policy is evaluated by the mean return $\bar{G}_{\phi+\sigma\epsilon_i}$ over multiple randomly sampled trajectories. Eventually, we are able to estimate the gradient of $\phi$ according to [NES](http://blog.otoro.net/2017/10/29/visual-evolution-strategies/) numerically ([Salimans et al, 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.03864)). While repeating this process, both the policy parameters $\theta$ and the loss function weights $\phi$ are being updated simultaneously to achieve higher returns.

$$
\phi \leftarrow \phi + \alpha_\text{out} \frac{1}{\sigma N} \sum_{i=1}^N \epsilon_i G_{\phi+\sigma\epsilon_i}
$$

其中 $\alpha_\text{out}$ 是外循环的学习率。

> where $\alpha_\text{out}$ is the learning rate of the outer loop.

在实践中，损失 $L_\phi$ 通过普通的策略梯度（例如 REINFORCE 或 PPO）替代损失 $L_\text{pg}$、$\hat{L} = (1-\alpha) L_\phi + \alpha L_\text{pg}$ 进行自举。权重 $\alpha$ 在训练期间从 1 逐渐退火到 0。在测试时，损失函数参数 $\phi$ 保持固定，并且通过计算历史经验的损失值来更新策略参数 $\theta$。

> In practice, the loss $L_\phi$ is bootstrapped with an ordinary policy gradient (such as REINFORCE or PPO) surrogate loss $L_\text{pg}$, $\hat{L} = (1-\alpha) L_\phi + \alpha L_\text{pg}$. The weight $\alpha$ is annealing from 1 to 0 gradually during training. At test time, the loss function parameter $\phi$ stays fixed and the loss value is computed over a history of experience to update the policy parameters $\theta$.

#### 元学习探索策略

> Meta-learning the Exploration Strategies

在强化学习中，[利用与探索](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-01-23-multi-armed-bandit/#exploitation-vs-exploration)的困境是一个关键问题。常见的探索方法包括ε-贪婪策略、动作上的随机噪声，或者在动作空间中内置随机性的随机策略。

> The [exploitation vs exploration](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-01-23-multi-armed-bandit/#exploitation-vs-exploration) dilemma is a critical problem in RL. Common ways to do exploration include ε-greedy, random noise on actions, or stochastic policy with built-in randomness on the action space.

**MAESN** ([Gupta 等人，2018](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/7776-meta-reinforcement-learning-of-structured-exploration-strategies.pdf)) 是一种从先验经验中学习结构化动作噪声的算法，以实现更好、更有效的探索。简单地在动作上添加随机噪声无法捕捉任务相关或时间相关的探索策略。MAESN 将策略修改为以每个任务的随机变量$z_i \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_i, \sigma_i)$为条件，对于第`i`个任务`M_i`，我们将得到策略$a \sim \pi_\theta(a\mid s, z_i)$。潜在变量`z_i`在一次回合中被采样一次并固定。直观地，潜在变量决定了一种行为（或技能）类型，这种类型在 rollout 开始时应更多地被探索，并且智能体将相应地调整其动作。策略参数和潜在空间都经过优化，以最大化总任务奖励。同时，策略学习利用潜在变量进行探索。

英文原文：MAESN ([Gupta et al, 2018](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/7776-meta-reinforcement-learning-of-structured-exploration-strategies.pdf)) is an algorithm to learn structured action noise from prior experience for better and more effective exploration. Simply adding random noise on actions cannot capture task-dependent or time-correlated exploration strategies. MAESN changes the policy to condition on a per-task random variable 

$z_i \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_i, \sigma_i)$, for `i` -th task `M_i`, so we would have a policy 

$a \sim \pi_\theta(a\mid s, z_i)$.
The latent variable `z_i` is sampled once and fixed during one episode. Intuitively, the latent variable determines one type of behavior (or skills) that should be explored more at the beginning of a rollout and the agent would adjust its actions accordingly. Both the policy parameters and latent space are optimized to maximize the total task rewards. In the meantime, the policy learns to make use of the latent variables for exploration.

此外，损失函数包含学习到的潜在变量与单位高斯先验之间的KL散度，$D_\text{KL}(\mathcal{N}(\mu_i, \sigma_i)|\mathcal{N}(0, \mathbf{I}))$。一方面，它限制了学习到的潜在空间与共同先验不会相距太远。另一方面，它为奖励函数创建了变分证据下界（[ELBO](http://users.umiacs.umd.edu/~xyang35/files/understanding-variational-lower.pdf)）。有趣的是，该论文发现每个任务的$(\mu_i, \sigma_i)$在收敛时通常接近先验。

> In addition,  the loss function includes a KL divergence between the learned latent variable and a unit Gaussian prior, $D_\text{KL}(\mathcal{N}(\mu_i, \sigma_i)|\mathcal{N}(0, \mathbf{I}))$. On one hand, it restricts the learned latent space not too far from a common prior. On the other hand, it creates the variational evidence lower bound ([ELBO](http://users.umiacs.umd.edu/~xyang35/files/understanding-variational-lower.pdf)) for the reward function. Interestingly the paper found that $(\mu_i, \sigma_i)$ for each task are usually close to the prior at convergence.

![The policy is conditioned on a latent variable variable $z\_i \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu, \sigma)$ that is sampled once every episode. Each task has different hyperparameters for the latent variable distribution, $(\mu\_i, \sigma\_i)$ and they are optimized in the outer loop. (Image source: Gupta et al, 2018 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/MAESN.png)

#### 幕时控制

> Episodic Control

强化学习的一个主要批评是其样本效率低下。强化学习需要大量的样本和小的学习步长来进行增量参数调整，以最大化泛化能力并避免灾难性遗忘早期学习内容（[Botvinick et al., 2019](https://www.cell.com/trends/cognitive-sciences/fulltext/S1364-6613(19)30061-0)）。

> A major criticism of RL is on its sample inefficiency. A large number of samples and small learning steps are required for incremental parameter adjustment in RL in order to maximize generalization and avoid catastrophic forgetting of earlier learning ([Botvinick et al., 2019](https://www.cell.com/trends/cognitive-sciences/fulltext/S1364-6613(19)30061-0)).

**情景控制** ([Lengyel & Dayan, 2008](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/3311-hippocampal-contributions-to-control-the-third-way.pdf)) 被提出作为一种解决方案，以避免遗忘并在训练时提高泛化能力，同时加快训练速度。它部分受到基于实例的[海马体](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hippocampus)学习假说的启发。

> **Episodic control** ([Lengyel & Dayan, 2008](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/3311-hippocampal-contributions-to-control-the-third-way.pdf)) is proposed as a solution to avoid forgetting and improve generalization while training at a faster speed. It is partially inspired by hypotheses on instance-based [hippocampal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hippocampus) learning.

一种*情景记忆*保留过去事件的明确记录，并直接使用这些记录作为做出新决策的参考点（即，就像[基于度量](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/#metric-based)的元学习）。在**MFEC**（无模型情景控制；[Blundell et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/abs/1606.04460)）中，记忆被建模为一个大表，将状态-动作对$(s, a)$存储为键，将相应的Q值$Q_\text{EC}(s, a)$存储为值。当接收到新的观测`s`时，Q值以非参数方式估计为前`k`个最相似样本的平均Q值：

英文原文：An *episodic memory* keeps explicit records of past events and uses these records directly as point of reference for making new decisions (i.e. just like [metric-based](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/#metric-based) meta-learning). In MFEC (Model-Free Episodic Control; [Blundell et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/abs/1606.04460)), the memory is modeled as a big table, storing the state-action pair 

$(s, a)$ as key and the corresponding Q-value 

$Q_\text{EC}(s, a)$ as value. When receiving a new observation `s`, the Q value is estimated in an non-parametric way as the average Q-value of top `k` most similar samples:

$$
\hat{Q}_\text{EC}(s, a) = 
\begin{cases}
Q_\text{EC}(s, a)                      & \text{if } (s,a) \in Q_\text{EC}, \\
\frac{1}{k} \sum_{i=1}^k Q(s^{(i)}, a) & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

其中$s^{(i)}, i=1, \dots, k$是前$k$个与状态$s$。然后选择产生最高估计Q值的动作。然后根据在$s_t$处收到的回报更新记忆表：

> where $s^{(i)}, i=1, \dots, k$ are top $k$ states with smallest distances to the state $s$. Then the action that yields the highest estimated Q value is selected. Then the memory table is updated according to the return received at $s_t$:

$$
Q_\text{EC}(s, a) \leftarrow
\begin{cases}
\max\{Q_\text{EC}(s_t, a_t), G_t\}  & \text{if } (s,a) \in Q_\text{EC}, \\
G_t                                 & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

作为一种表格型强化学习方法，MFEC 存在内存消耗大和难以在相似状态之间泛化的问题。第一个问题可以通过 LRU 缓存解决。受 [基于度量](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/#metric-based) 的元学习，特别是匹配网络（[Vinyals et al., 2016](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/6385-matching-networks-for-one-shot-learning.pdf)）的启发，泛化问题在后续算法 **NEC**（神经情景控制；[Pritzel et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.01988)）中得到了改进。

> As a tabular RL method, MFEC suffers from large memory consumption and a lack of ways to generalize among similar states. The first one can be fixed with an LRU cache. Inspired by [metric-based](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-11-30-meta-learning/#metric-based) meta-learning, especially Matching Networks ([Vinyals et al., 2016](http://papers.nips.cc/paper/6385-matching-networks-for-one-shot-learning.pdf)), the generalization problem is improved in a follow-up algorithm, **NEC** (Neural Episodic Control; [Pritzel et al., 2016](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.01988)).

NEC 中的情景记忆是一个可微分神经字典（**DND**），其中键是输入图像像素的卷积嵌入向量，值存储估计的 Q 值。给定一个查询键，输出是与最相似键的值的加权和，其中权重是查询键与字典中选定键之间的归一化核度量。这听起来像是一种硬 [注意力](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-06-24-attention/) 机制。

> The episodic memory in NEC is a Differentiable Neural Dictionary (**DND**), where the key is a convolutional embedding vector of input image pixels and the value stores estimated Q value. Given an inquiry key, the output is a weighted sum of values of top similar keys, where the weight is a normalized kernel measure between the query key and the selected key in the dictionary. This sounds like a hard [attention](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2018-06-24-attention/) machanism.

![Fig. 6 Illustrations of episodic memory module in NEC and two operations on a differentiable neural dictionary. (Image source: Pritzel et al., 2016 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/neural-episodic-control.png)

此外，**情景式LSTM** ([Ritter 等人，2018](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.09692)) 通过DND情景记忆增强了基本的LSTM架构，该记忆将任务上下文嵌入存储为键，将LSTM单元状态存储为值。存储的隐藏状态通过LSTM内部相同的门控机制被检索并直接添加到当前单元状态中：

> Further, **Episodic LSTM** ([Ritter et al., 2018](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.09692)) enhances the basic LSTM architecture with a DND episodic memory, which stores task context embeddings as keys and the LSTM cell states as values. The stored hidden states are retrieved and added directly to the current cell state through the same gating mechanism within LSTM:

![Illustration of the episodic LSTM architecture. The additional structure of episodic memory is in bold. (Image source: Ritter et al., 2018 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/episodic-LSTM.png)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathbf{c}_t &= \mathbf{i}_t \circ \mathbf{c}_\text{in} + \mathbf{f}_t \circ \mathbf{c}_{t-1} + \color{green}{\mathbf{r}_t \circ \mathbf{c}_\text{ep}} &\\
\mathbf{i}_t &= \sigma(\mathbf{W}_{i} \cdot [\mathbf{h}_{t-1}, \mathbf{x}_t] + \mathbf{b}_i) & \scriptstyle{\text{; input gate}} \\
\mathbf{f}_t &= \sigma(\mathbf{W}_{f} \cdot [\mathbf{h}_{t-1}, \mathbf{x}_t] + \mathbf{b}_f) & \scriptstyle{\text{; forget gate}} \\
\color{green}{\mathbf{r}_t} & \color{green}{=} \color{green}{\sigma(\mathbf{W}_{r} \cdot [\mathbf{h}_{t-1}, \mathbf{x}_t] + \mathbf{b}_r)} & \scriptstyle{\text{; reinstatement gate}}
\end{aligned}
$$

其中 $\mathbf{c}_t$ 和 $\mathbf{h}_t$ 分别是时间 $t$ 的隐藏状态和单元状态；$\mathbf{i}_t$、$\mathbf{f}_t$ 和 $\mathbf{r}_t$ 分别是输入门、遗忘门和恢复门；$\mathbf{c}_\text{ep}$ 是从情景记忆中检索到的单元状态。新添加的情景记忆组件以绿色标记。

> where $\mathbf{c}_t$ and $\mathbf{h}_t$ are hidden and cell state at time $t$; $\mathbf{i}_t$, $\mathbf{f}_t$ and $\mathbf{r}_t$ are input, forget and reinstatement gates, respectively; $\mathbf{c}_\text{ep}$ is the retrieved cell state from episodic memory. The newly added episodic memory components are marked in green.

这种架构通过基于上下文的检索为先前的经验提供了捷径。同时，将任务相关的经验明确地保存在外部记忆中可以避免遗忘。在论文中，所有的实验都手动设计了上下文向量。如何为更自由形式的任务构建有效且高效的任务上下文嵌入格式将是一个有趣的话题。

> This architecture provides a shortcut to the prior experience through context-based retrieval. Meanwhile, explicitly saving the task-dependent experience in an external memory avoids forgetting. In the paper, all the experiments have manually designed context vectors. How to construct an effective and efficient format of task context embeddings for more free-formed tasks would be an interesting topic.

总的来说，情景控制的能力受限于环境的复杂性。在现实世界的任务中，智能体很少会重复访问完全相同的状态，因此正确编码状态至关重要。学习到的嵌入空间将观测数据压缩到低维空间中，同时，在这个空间中接近的两个状态预计会需要相似的策略。

> Overall the capacity of episodic control is limited by the complexity of the environment. It is very rare for an agent to repeatedly visit exactly the same states in a real-world task, so properly encoding the states is critical. The learned embedding space compresses the observation data into a lower dimension space and, in the meantime, two states being close in this space are expected to demand similar strategies.

### 训练任务获取

> Training Task Acquisition

在三个关键组件中，如何设计一个合适的任务分布是研究较少且可能对元强化学习本身最具特异性的一个。如[上文](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#formulation)所述，每个任务都是一个MDP：$M_i = \langle \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P_i, R_i \rangle \in \mathcal{M}$。我们可以通过修改以下内容来构建MDP的分布：

> Among three key components, how to design a proper distribution of tasks is the less studied and probably the most specific one to meta-RL itself. As described [above](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#formulation), each task is a MDP: $M_i = \langle \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P_i, R_i \rangle \in \mathcal{M}$. We can build a distribution of MDPs by modifying:

• *奖励配置*：在不同任务中，相同的行为可能会根据$R_i$获得不同的奖励。

• 或者，*环境*：转移函数 $P_i$ 可以通过初始化具有不同状态间偏移量的环境来重塑。

英文原文：

• The *reward configuration*: Among different tasks, same behavior might get rewarded differently according to $R_i$.

• Or, the *environment*: The transition function $P_i$ can be reshaped by initializing the environment with varying shifts between states.

#### 通过域随机化生成任务

> Task Generation by Domain Randomization

在模拟器中随机化参数是获取具有修改过的转移函数的任务的简单方法。如果有兴趣进一步了解，请查看我的上一篇[文章](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-05-05-domain-randomization/)，内容关于**领域随机化**。

> Randomizing parameters in a simulator is an easy way to obtain tasks with modified transition functions. If interested in learning further, check my last [post](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-05-05-domain-randomization/) on **domain randomization**.

#### 环境生成中的进化算法

> Evolutionary Algorithm on Environment Generation

[进化算法](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolutionary_algorithm)是一种受自然选择启发的无梯度启发式优化方法。解决方案的种群遵循评估、选择、繁殖和变异的循环。最终，好的解决方案得以存活并被选中。

> [Evolutionary algorithm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolutionary_algorithm) is a gradient-free heuristic-based optimization method, inspired by natural selection. A population of solutions follows a loop of evaluation, selection, reproduction, and mutation. Eventually, good solutions survive and thus get selected.

**POET** ([Wang et al, 2019](https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.01753)) 是一个基于进化算法的框架，它试图在问题本身被解决的同时生成任务。POET 的实现仅专门为简单的 2D [双足步行器](https://gym.openai.com/envs/BipedalWalkerHardcore-v2/)环境设计，但指出了一个有趣的方向。值得注意的是，进化算法在深度学习中也有一些引人注目的应用，例如 [EPG](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#meta-learning-the-loss-function) 和 PBT (Population-Based Training; [ Jaderberg et al, 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.09846))。

> **POET** ([Wang et al, 2019](https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.01753)), a framework based on the evolutionary algorithm, attempts to generate tasks while the problems themselves are being solved. The implementation of POET is only specifically designed for a simple 2D [bipedal walker](https://gym.openai.com/envs/BipedalWalkerHardcore-v2/) environment but points out an interesting direction. It is noteworthy that the evolutionary algorithm has had some compelling applications in Deep Learning like [EPG](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#meta-learning-the-loss-function) and PBT (Population-Based Training; [ Jaderberg et al, 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.09846)).

![An example bipedal walking environment (top) and an overview of POET (bottom). (Image source: POET blog post )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/POET.png)

2D 双足行走环境正在演变：从简单的平坦表面到更具挑战性的路径，其中可能包含间隙、树桩和崎岖地形。POET 将环境挑战的生成与智能体的优化结合起来，以便 (a) 选择能够解决当前挑战的智能体，以及 (b) 演化出可解决的环境。该算法维护一个*环境-智能体对*列表，并重复以下步骤：

> The 2D bipedal walking environment is evolving: from a simple flat surface to a much more difficult trail with potential gaps, stumps, and rough terrains. POET pairs the generation of environmental challenges and the optimization of agents together so as to (a) select agents that can resolve current challenges and (b) evolve environments to be solvable. The algorithm maintains a list of *environment-agent pairs* and repeats the following:

1. *变异*：从当前活跃的环境中生成新环境。请注意，这里的变异操作类型是专为双足步行器创建的，新环境将需要一套新的配置。
2. *优化*：在各自的环境中训练配对的智能体。
3. *选择*: 定期尝试将当前智能体从一个环境转移到另一个环境。复制并更新每个环境中表现最佳的智能体。直觉是，在一个环境中学习到的技能可能对不同的环境有所帮助。

> • *Mutation*: Generate new environments from currently active environments. Note that here types of mutation operations are created just for bipedal walker and a new environment would demand a new set of configurations.
> • *Optimization*: Train paired agents within their respective environments.
> • *Selection*: Periodically attempt to transfer current agents from one environment to another. Copy and update the best performing agent for every environment. The intuition is that skills learned in one environment might be helpful for a different environment.

上述过程与[PBT](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.09846)非常相似，但PBT是变异和演化超参数。在某种程度上，POET正在进行[域随机化](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-05-05-domain-randomization/)，因为所有的间隙、树桩和地形粗糙度都由一些随机化概率参数控制。与DR不同的是，智能体并非一次性暴露在完全随机的困难环境中，而是通过进化算法配置的课程逐步学习。

> The procedure above is quite similar to [PBT](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.09846), but PBT mutates and evolves hyperparameters instead. To some extent, POET is doing [domain randomization](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-05-05-domain-randomization/), as all the gaps, stumps and terrain roughness are controlled by some randomization probability parameters. Different from DR, the agents are not exposed to a fully randomized difficult environment all at once, but instead they are learning gradually with a curriculum configured by the evolutionary algorithm.

#### 随机奖励学习

> Learning with Random Rewards

没有奖励函数$R$的MDP被称为*受控马尔可夫过程*（CMP）。给定一个预定义的CMP，$\langle \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P\rangle$，我们可以通过生成一系列奖励函数$\mathcal{R}$来获取各种任务，这些奖励函数鼓励训练出有效的元学习策略。

> An MDP without a reward function $R$ is known as a *Controlled Markov process* (CMP). Given a predefined CMP, $\langle \mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P\rangle$, we can acquire a variety of tasks by generating a collection of reward functions $\mathcal{R}$ that encourage the training of an effective meta-learning policy.

[Gupta 等人 (2018)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.04640)提出了两种无监督方法，用于在CMP背景下扩展任务分布。假设每个任务都关联着一个潜在变量$z \sim p(z)$，它参数化/决定了一个奖励函数：$r_z(s) = \log D(z|s)$，其中使用“判别器”函数$D(.)$从状态中提取潜在变量。该论文描述了两种构建判别器函数的方法：

> [Gupta et al. (2018)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.04640) proposed two unsupervised approaches  for growing the task distribution in the context of CMP. Assuming there is an underlying latent variable $z \sim p(z)$ associated with every task, it parameterizes/determines a reward function: $r_z(s) = \log D(z|s)$, where a “discriminator” function $D(.)$ is used to extract the latent variable from the state. The paper described two ways to construct a discriminator function:

• 对判别器的随机权重 $\phi_\text{rand}$ 进行采样, $D_{\phi_\text{rand}}(z \mid s)$。

• 学习一个判别器函数，以鼓励多样性驱动的探索。该方法在另一篇姊妹论文“DIAYN”中进行了更详细的介绍（[Eysenbach et al., 2018](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.06070)）。

英文原文：

• Sample random weights $\phi_\text{rand}$ of the discriminator, $D_{\phi_\text{rand}}(z \mid s)$.

• Learn a discriminator function to encourage diversity-driven exploration. This method is introduced in more details in another sister paper “DIAYN” ([Eysenbach et al., 2018](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.06070)).

DIAYN，即“多样性就是你所需要的一切”，是一个鼓励策略在没有奖励函数的情况下学习有用技能的框架。它明确地将潜在变量建模为$z$作为一个*技能*嵌入，并使策略以...为条件$z$除了状态$s$，$\pi_\theta(a \mid s, z)$。（好的，这部分与[MAESN](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#meta-learning-the-exploration-strategies)毫不奇怪，因为这些论文来自同一个研究组。）DIAYN 的设计受到以下几个假设的启发：

> DIAYN, short for “Diversity is all you need”, is a framework to encourage a policy to learn useful skills without a reward function. It explicitly models the latent variable $z$ as a *skill* embedding and makes the policy conditioned on $z$ in addition to state $s$, $\pi_\theta(a \mid s, z)$. (Ok, this part is same as [MAESN](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/#meta-learning-the-exploration-strategies) unsurprisingly, as the papers are from the same group.) The design of DIAYN is motivated by a few hypotheses:

• 技能应该多样化并导致访问不同的状态。→ 最大化状态和技能之间的互信息，$I(S; Z)$

• 技能应该通过状态而不是动作来区分。→ 最小化动作和技能之间的互信息，以状态为条件 $I(A; Z \mid S)$

英文原文：

• Skills should be diverse and lead to visitations of different states. → maximize the mutual information between states and skills, $I(S; Z)$

• Skills should be distinguishable by states, not actions. → minimize the mutual information between actions and skills, conditioned on states $I(A; Z \mid S)$

要最大化的目标函数如下，其中还添加了策略熵以鼓励多样性：

> The objective function to maximize is as follows, where the policy entropy is also added to encourage diversity:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathcal{F}(\theta) 
&= I(S; Z) + H[A \mid S] - I(A; Z \mid S) &  \\
&= (H(Z) - H(Z \mid S)) + H[A \mid S] - (H[A\mid S] - H[A\mid S, Z]) & \\
&= H[A\mid S, Z] \color{green}{- H(Z \mid S) + H(Z)} & \\
&= H[A\mid S, Z] + \mathbb{E}_{z\sim p(z), s\sim\rho(s)}[\log p(z \mid s)] - \mathbb{E}_{z\sim p(z)}[\log p(z)] & \scriptstyle{\text{; can infer skills from states & p(z) is diverse.}} \\
&\ge H[A\mid S, Z] + \mathbb{E}_{z\sim p(z), s\sim\rho(s)}[\color{red}{\log D_\phi(z \mid s) - \log p(z)}] & \scriptstyle{\text{; according to Jensen's inequality; pseudo-reward in red.}}
\end{aligned}
$$

其中 $I(.)$ 是互信息，$H[.]$ 是熵度量。我们无法整合所有状态来计算 $p(z \mid s)$，因此用 $D_\phi(z \mid s)$ 来近似它——这就是多样性驱动的判别器函数。

> where $I(.)$ is mutual information and $H[.]$ is entropy measure. We cannot integrate all states to compute $p(z \mid s)$, so approximate it with $D_\phi(z \mid s)$ — that is the diversity-driven discriminator function.

![DIAYN Algorithm. (Image source: Eysenbach et al., 2019 )](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2019-06-23-meta-rl/DIAYN.png)

一旦判别器函数被学习，为训练采样一个新的 MDP 就很简单了：首先，采样一个潜在变量 $z \sim p(z)$ 并构建一个奖励函数 $r_z(s) = \log(D(z \vert s))$。将奖励函数与预定义的 CMP 配对会创建一个新的 MDP。

> Once the discriminator function is learned, sampling a new MDP for training is strainght-forward: First, sample a latent variable, $z \sim p(z)$ and construct a reward function $r_z(s) = \log(D(z \vert s))$. Pairing the reward function with a predefined CMP creates a new MDP.
